Efraín Pérez Delgado - Project Site Plan

1. Site Name

Madrid Temple Patron Housing
Domain: churchofjesuschrist.org/temples/details/madrid-spain-temple/patron-housing

2. Site Purpose

This site allows people wanting to come to the Madrid Temple to book acomodation in the Madrid Temple Patron Housing for themselves, either as individuals or as families

3. Scenarios

  • What is the price per person and night?
  • Who qualifies to stay in the Patron Housing?
  • How long can my stay be?
  • What are the check-in and check-out times?
  • What are the room types available?

4. Color Schema

The site will follow a similar color schema to the official temple pages

  • Headings: #000 (black)
  • Background: #fff (white)
  • Primary Accent Color: #006184
  • Link accent color: #157493

5. Typography

The site will use sans fonts throught. Montserrat for headings and Lato for paragraphs
